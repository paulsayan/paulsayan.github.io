Ranked 324-th (Top 0.3% of 99,473 participants) in TCS CodeVita 2017 (Global Coding Contest)

Created on August 11, 2017

2017

In 2017, I competed in TCS CodeVita, the world’s largest competitive programming contest as recognized by the Guinness World Records. Facing a rigorous 6-hour challenge designed to test advanced algorithmic thinking and real-world problem-solving, I secured a Global Rank of 324 out of 99,473 participants. This achievement placed me in the top 0.3% of competitors worldwide, demonstrating my ability to deliver highly optimized code under intense pressure, and directly earned me a shortlist for a TCS technical interview.
